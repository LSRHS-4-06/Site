There are so many places to stay!

There are many places to stay when you arrive at the castle. The most popular tends to be the castle itself! Book these trips with the help of your travel agent or a travel site!

  • Stay at Hogwarts -- 500 pounds a night
  • Stay at Hogsmeade -- apartment: 50 pounds a night / house: 100 pounds a night
  • Camp in Forbidden Forest -- 10 pound admission fee
  • SPECIAL OFFER: Stay at Hagrid's hut -- 3000 pounds (only available for one family at a time)
    • All of these lodging options (excluding staying at Hogwarts castle) require an addition fee should you want to access any of the amenities on Hogwarts grounds. To find out what these are, visit the Attractions page
