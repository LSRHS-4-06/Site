There are so many things to do!

These are some of the most popular attractions at Hogwarts!

  • Visit Ollivander's wand shop and get your own customized wand!
  • Go see the Sorting Hat and see if you are a Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, or Slytherin.
  • Go see a quidditch game!
  • Drink butterbeer and eat sweets at Hogsmeade!
  • Explore the depths of the castle!
  • Visit the remains of the infamous Battle of Hogwarts!
  • Hunt for Horcruxes!
