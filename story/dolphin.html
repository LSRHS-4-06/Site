Daniel the Dolphin


Blair flew high and low, until she came across the majestic ocean. She saw a dolphin swimming in the waves and flew over to him. “Hi! I am trying to catch the sun, can you help me?” she asked. “I would love to! I love the sun too! But I live underneath the waves, so I cannot help you in your search. Good luck!” he said. So Blair flew away.
