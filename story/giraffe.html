Grady the Giraffe


Blair found a giraffe some time later. “Hi,” she said. “Can you help me catch the sun?” The giraffe turned and blinked at her. “I really want to, but I am too afraid of leaving home. I am afraid of other worlds, and I don’t think I will survive.” Blair nodded in understanding, and flew away.
