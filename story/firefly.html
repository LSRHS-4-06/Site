Freedom the Firefly


All of a sudden, she heard a buzzing behind her. She turned to see a firefly coming towards her. “My name is Frankie,” he said. “I heard that you have been trying to catch the sun!” Blair nodded sadly. “I cannot catch the sun though,” she said. “That’s okay, don’t be afraid. I’ll be your light,” he said. His bulb flickered behind him. Blair smiled. Finally, she thought.
