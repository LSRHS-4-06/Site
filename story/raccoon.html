Randy the Raccoon


Blair flew for a long time, until she found a raccoon. “Hi,” she said. “Can you help me chase the sun?” The raccoon stared at her with angry eyes. “No,” he said. “My only time for food comes when the sun goes down. Chasing the sun is a waste of time.” Dejected once more, Brady flittered off into the distance.
